About Indian Galleries

At Indian Galleries, lighting and decor are more than elements of a space they are expressions of character, craftsmanship, and culture. What began with a focus on premium lamps and lighting has grown into a diverse collection of handmade metal, wood, and décor articles created with care and intention.

Our work is shaped by deep respect for the artisans and communities who bring each piece to life. As active members of Fair Trade Forum India, we follow practices that uphold fairness, transparency, and responsibility throughout our craft. This reflects in the way we source materials, support traditional skills, and ensure that every product contributes to a more sustainable and thoughtful way of living.

From table lamps, chandeliers, and pendant lights to handcrafted home décor, wooden articles, and contemporary metal designs, every item is made to offer both beauty and lasting value. Each piece carries a story of skilled hands, mindful production, and a commitment to creating products that enhance spaces while honoring the people behind them.

Indian Galleries continues to be a preferred choice for customers across India and around the world who appreciate authentic craftsmanship, ethical processes, and timeless design

Founder

Late Mr. Sandeep Srivastava was the true visionary behind Indian Galleries. His passion for Indian handicrafts and lighting design laid the foundation for the company. Through his dedication and creativity, he aimed to bring authentic Indian craftsmanship into every home.

His legacy continues to inspire the team, shaping the core values of the brand even today.

CEO

Mrs. Pooja Srivastava is the dynamic CEO of Indian Galleries. She transformed her late husband's vision into a growing brand, combining creativity, resilience, and strong leadership.

Under her guidance, Indian Galleries has grown into a trusted name, blending tradition with modern elegance in lighting and decor.

CFO/CTO

Mr. Akshat Srivastava serves as the CFO/CTO of Indian Galleries, overseeing the company’s operational strategies and ensuring excellence in field execution and planning.

With a strong focus on coordination and quality control, he plays a vital role in maintaining efficiency and customer satisfaction across all departments.

Our Mission
Our mission is to connect global consumers with eco-friendly, ethically crafted décor, wall artware, lightware, and kitchenware, celebrating the spirit of Indian artistry in every product. We aim to be a leading global name in the handicrafts industry, known for quality, authenticity, and social responsibility, while actively contributing to the economic upliftment of rural communities.
Our Vision
Our vision is to become a globally admired brand that preserves and promotes India’s rich cultural heritage through authentic, high-quality handmade handicrafts that inspire beauty, sustainability, and tradition in every home. We are committed to empowering local artisans across India by providing sustainable livelihood opportunities and showcasing their craftsmanship on both national and international platforms.
Our Aim
Our objectives include expanding market presence through e-commerce and retail, collaborating with artisans by 2030 to ensure fair wages and skill development, launching a curated product line rooted in Indian tradition, achieving complete customer satisfaction through ethical practices, promoting innovation while preserving heritage, and conducting workshops and exhibitions that bring Indian crafts to the world stage.
