PHF विद्यापीठ – निःशुल्क शिक्षा एवं भोजन

Indian Galleries is honoured to support PHF विद्यापीठ (Parag Human Foundation Trust, Moradabad), an initiative dedicated to providing निःशुल्क शिक्षा, किताबें, बैग, एवं भोजन to underprivileged children aged 4 to 6 years.

The mission of PHF Vidhyapeeth is to ensure that every child—regardless of financial background—receives the opportunity to learn, grow, and build a brighter future. Along with quality education, children are provided with nutritious meals daily, ensuring both physical and intellectual nourishment.

This initiative reflects our commitment to empowering young minds and uplifting communities through education, care, and equal opportunities.

वैदिक वानप्रस्थ आश्रम – मुरादाबाद

Indian Galleries contributes regularly to वैदिक वानप्रस्थ आश्रम, Supporting elderly residents with care, comfort, and essential needs.We believe in giving back to those who have dedicated their lives to society. Our efforts focus on creating a safe, dignified, and nurturing environment for senior citizens.We aim to bring warmth, compassion, and hope through every small act of service. Together, we strive to make their golden years more meaningful and joyful.

दिव्य शक्ति डे-केयर होम

We proudly support Divya Shakti Day-Care Home, An initiative dedicated to empowering individuals with special needs through love, care, and meaningful engagement.We strive to create an inclusive environment that nurtures confidence and independence. Our support programs focus on enhancing life skills, emotional well-being, and social interaction.Together, we aim to build a compassionate community where every individual feels valued and respected.
