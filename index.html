About Us

At Indian Galleries, we believe lighting does more than just brighten a room – it transforms spaces, creates ambiance, and showcases individuality.

As a trusted name in premium lamps, lighting, and metal handicrafts across India and internationally, we specialize in offering an extensive selection of lighting solutions that combine functionality with exceptional design.

Each piece reflects our commitment to craftsmanship, quality, and innovation, making Indian Galleries a preferred choice for discerning customers seeking both beauty and utility. We invite you to explore our offerings and discover lighting solutions that redefine your spaces.

Founded on January 1, 2020 by Mrs. Pooja Srivastav, an expert artist and craftswoman, and managed with dedication by Mr. Akshat Srivastava, our team strives to bring excellence to every home and project we serve.
