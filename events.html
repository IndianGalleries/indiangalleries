FTF-I National Convention 2025

25 Years of Promoting Fair Trade in India

Fair Trade Movement

We are honored to participate in theFTF-I National Convention 2025,celebrating 25 years of promoting fair trade in India. Fair Trade Forum – India (FTF-I) is a non-profit organization committed to building sustainable livelihoods, empowering marginalized artisans, and promoting ethical trade practices across the country.

Through collaboration, advocacy, and responsible business practices, the fair-trade movement supports fair wages, dignified working conditions,and long-term opportunities for artisan communities.Being part of this journey reflects our dedication to ethical craftsmanship and sustainable production.

From thoughtful beginnings to meaningful connections, every step we take is guided by shared learning, community empowerment, and responsible trade.

  • 12–14 December 2025
  • fairtradeforum.org.in
