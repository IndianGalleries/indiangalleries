Fair Trade Forum – India (FTF-I)

Fair Trade Forum–India (FTF-I) is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development, social equity, and ethical trade practices across India.

FTF-I empowers marginalized artisans and producers by enabling access to fair wages, dignified working conditions, and long-term growth opportunities ensuring a more inclusive and just economic ecosystem.

Through collaboration, advocacy, and capacity-building, FTF-I champions transparency, accountability, and respect strengthening the fair-trade movement and uplifting communities across the nation.

Jaquar Group

Conceived in 1960 and headquartered in Manesar, the Jaquar Group is a global leader in Complete Bathroom and Lighting Solutions. Built on uncompromising quality, world-class engineering, and cutting-edge design, Jaquar caters to luxury, premium, and value segments across the world.

With 8 state-of-the-art manufacturing units across India and South Korea and a presence in 55+ countries, Jaquar delivers millions of bath fittings, sanitaryware, and lighting products annually setting global benchmarks in innovation and performance.

Recognized as India’s most trusted bathroom brand and a Super Brand for 12 consecutive years, Jaquar continues to lead the industry in technology, sustainability, and customer excellence.

All India Women’s Conference (AIWC)

Founded in 1927 and registered in 1930, the All India Women’s Conference (AIWC) is one of India’s oldest and most respected organizations committed to the upliftment, empowerment, and welfare of women and children.

AIWC works to ensure that women across India receive education, equal rights, safety, dignity, and opportunities for leadership. The organization actively supports social reform, skill development, and awareness programs to eliminate violence and discrimination.

Standing strong for nearly a century, AIWC continues its mission to build a society where every woman is empowered, educated, and treated as an equal contributor to the nation’s progress.
